Suzuki Ignis Advertisement

Instagram advertisement created in the class "GRDE 129: Introduction to Graphic Design," presenting the Suzuki Ignis by utilizing the software Adobe Illustrator and tools such as scaling, cropping for getting the best-suited image size, dragging and dropping for the best position, and stylish fonts to enhance the appearance. A lot was learned from this assignment in relation to using Adobe Illustrator, and it was a great experience for exploring the creative side of my mind. .
