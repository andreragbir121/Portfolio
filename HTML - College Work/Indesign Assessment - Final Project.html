Mythic Runner(Final Project)

Final project accomplished for the class"GRDE 129: Introduction to Graphic Design", with the use of Adobe InDesign, I decided to design an InDesign of a game that I was creating, with the use of tools such as scaling for proper sizes of the images, sophisticated fonts for attractivity, and color droppers for applying blending colors to fonts to complement the images. The game was generally an endless runner with aliens in mythical places..
